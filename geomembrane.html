ANVIX™ Geomembrane liners are engineered for reliable waterproofing, chemical resistance and long-term durability across a wide range of water containment and environmental applications.

Available in both HDPE and LLDPE formulations, our geomembranes are manufactured from premium virgin resin enhanced with carbon black, antioxidants and UV stabilisers — delivering consistent performance in demanding field conditions.
