ANVIX™
Low Tunnel Films
by Blacksoil International

ANVIX™ Low Tunnel Films are designed to provide reliable crop protection, improved growing conditions and enhanced productivity during the early stages of cultivation.

Widely used for vegetables, melons, strawberries, watermelons and other high-value crops, low tunnel films help create a stable microclimate that promotes faster establishment, healthier growth and improved crop quality.

Our product range focuses on proven market solutions that meet the requirements of growers across different climates and cultivation systems.

ANVIX™ Low Tunnel Films — Product Range
4 Film Types
01
ANVIX™ Standard Film
A cost-effective solution for general crop protection and early-season cultivation. Manufactured from high-quality polyethylene resins, it provides excellent transparency, mechanical strength and weather resistance for short-term agricultural applications. The film helps protect crops from wind, rain and environmental stress while allowing maximum natural light transmission.
THICKNESS RANGE: 15 µm – 25 µm
Features
  • High light transmission
  • Good mechanical strength
  • Excellent transparency
  • Easy installation
  • Cost-effective solution
Benefits
  • Faster crop establishment
  • Improved plant growth
  • Reduced environmental stress
  • Higher crop uniformity
  • Economical cultivation cost
Suitable Applications
  • Watermelon
  • Melon
  • Strawberry
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Early-season crops
02
ANVIX™ Thermic Film
Incorporates specialised Infrared (IR) retention technology designed to reduce heat loss during the night. By retaining thermal radiation within the tunnel environment, the film helps maintain more stable night-time temperatures and reduces sudden temperature fluctuations. Compared with conventional polyethylene films, thermic films can improve night temperatures by approximately 2–4°C under suitable conditions.
THICKNESS RANGE: 30 µm – 80 µm
Features
  • IR heat retention technology
  • Improved night-time temperature stability
  • Reduced temperature fluctuations
  • Enhanced crop protection during cooler periods
  • Suitable for early-season cultivation
Benefits
  • Reduced frost risk
  • Faster crop development
  • Earlier harvesting
  • Improved crop quality
  • Increased productivity
Suitable Applications
  • Strawberry
  • Tomato
  • Pepper
  • Early-season vegetables
  • Cold-climate crops
03
ANVIX™ Cooling Film
Engineered with PAR and NIR management technology to reduce excessive heat build-up within the tunnel environment. By filtering near-infrared radiation, the film helps maintain cooler temperatures during hot weather conditions, improving plant comfort and reducing heat stress. Suitable for tropical and subtropical growing environments where overheating is a critical challenge.
THICKNESS RANGE: 20 µm – 50 µm
Features
  • PAR and NIR management technology
  • Reduced heat accumulation
  • Improved temperature control
  • Enhanced crop comfort
  • Better environmental stability
Benefits
  • Lower plant stress
  • Improved crop quality
  • Better summer performance
  • Reduced overheating risk
  • Enhanced productivity in hot climates
Suitable Applications
  • Lettuce
  • Herbs
  • Cucumber
  • Summer vegetables
  • Tropical crops
04
ANVIX™ Perforated Film
Features strategically designed perforations that improve air circulation and moisture management within the tunnel. The perforation system helps regulate temperature, reduce condensation and maintain healthier growing conditions while still providing protection against environmental factors. Available in various perforation patterns depending on crop requirements.
THICKNESS RANGE: 15 µm – 25 µm
Features
  • Enhanced ventilation
  • Improved moisture control
  • Reduced condensation
  • Better temperature regulation
  • Optimised tunnel environment
Benefits
  • Healthier crop development
  • Reduced disease pressure
  • Improved air circulation
  • Lower humidity levels
  • Better crop quality
Suitable Applications
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Herbs
  • Carrots
  • Radish
  • Crops requiring ventilation
