ANVIX™
LLDPE Geomembrane
by Blacksoil International

ANVIX™ LLDPE Geomembrane is manufactured from premium linear low-density polyethylene resin, enhanced with carbon black, antioxidants and UV stabilisers for long-term outdoor performance.

Its superior flexibility, elongation and puncture resistance make it ideal for projects where ground movement, uneven surfaces and stress cracking resistance are critical.

Designed for demanding containment applications, ANVIX™ LLDPE Geomembrane provides reliable waterproofing, chemical resistance and long-term durability across a wide range of environmental conditions.

Why Choose LLDPE Geomembrane?
01
Better Flexibility Than HDPE
LLDPE geomembrane provides significantly higher flexibility and elongation, allowing the membrane to conform to uneven terrain and complex site conditions.
02
Improved Puncture Resistance
The flexible molecular structure helps absorb impact and reduces the risk of puncture damage during installation.
03
Superior Stress Crack Resistance
LLDPE performs exceptionally well in applications where long-term movement and deformation may occur.
04
Reliable Long-Term Containment
Combined with UV stabilisers and antioxidant packages, LLDPE geomembrane delivers durable waterproofing performance for demanding containment applications.
Quality Assurance
Standards & Testing
ANVIX™ LLDPE Geomembrane is manufactured and tested in accordance with internationally recognised geosynthetic standards, ensuring consistent quality and reliable field performance.
GRI GM17
Geosynthetic Research Institute Specification
GM17 is the industry-recognised specification for LLDPE geomembranes, covering minimum requirements for thickness, tensile properties, tear resistance, puncture resistance and carbon black content.
ASTM
ASTM Test Methods
Material properties are evaluated using established ASTM test methods, including tensile properties, tear resistance, puncture resistance, density and dimensional stability.
QA / QC
Quality Control Procedures
Each production batch is monitored for thickness uniformity, surface quality and mechanical properties to ensure consistent compliance with specification.
UV / OIT
Oxidative Induction Time Testing
OIT testing verifies the effectiveness of antioxidant packages, supporting long-term durability claims for outdoor installations.
