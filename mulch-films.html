ANVIX™
Mulch Films
by Blacksoil International

Mulch films are essential tools for modern crop production, helping growers improve yield, conserve water, suppress weeds, and maintain stable soil conditions. From conventional and biodegradable solutions to durable and reflective films, each product is designed to meet specific agricultural challenges and growing environments.

Selected ANVIX™ mulch film grades incorporate advanced metallocene technology, enabling stronger, lighter and more efficient film structures without compromising field durability.
