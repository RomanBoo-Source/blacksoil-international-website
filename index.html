Company Profile
Who We Are

BLACKSOIL INTERNATIONAL is a Malaysian-based materials company dedicated to the development and supply of advanced polymer film solutions for agriculture, water management and environmental protection.

Under its flagship ANVIX™ brand, the company offers a diversified portfolio of agricultural mulch films, greenhouse coverings, silage preservation solutions, aquaculture liners and geomembrane products designed to enhance efficiency, durability and field performance.

With a commitment to quality, innovation and long-term customer value, BLACKSOIL INTERNATIONAL supports growers, distributors, contractors and industrial partners with reliable material solutions tailored for modern industries worldwide.
