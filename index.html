06 / How We Work

From application to field support.

From understanding your application to long-term field support, we work closely with our partners to deliver the right film solution for every project.

Step 01

Understand Your Application

We begin by understanding your crop, cultivation method, climate conditions and application to recommend the most suitable film solution.

Step 02

Define the Right Performance

Together, we determine the required film specifications, functional additives, UV warranty and performance features to match your growing requirements.

Step 03

Precision Manufacturing

Your selected film solution is produced under controlled manufacturing processes with consistent formulation and strict production quality management.

Step 04

Verified Before Delivery

Every shipment is verified against the agreed product specifications and relevant international testing standards before export.

Step 05

Long-Term Partnership

Our commitment continues after delivery. We work closely with our distributors and customers to monitor product performance and provide prompt technical support whenever needed.

07 / Company Profile

Who we are.

Blacksoil International is a Malaysian-based materials company dedicated to the development and supply of advanced polymer film solutions for agriculture, water management, and environmental protection.

Under its flagship ANVIX™ brand, the company offers a diversified portfolio of mulch films, greenhouse coverings, silage preservation solutions, aquaculture liners, and geomembrane products designed to enhance efficiency, durability, and field performance.

With a commitment to quality, innovation, and long-term customer value, we support growers, distributors, contractors, and industrial partners with reliable material solutions tailored for modern industries worldwide.
