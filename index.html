Who We Are
BLACKSOIL INTERNATIONAL is a Malaysian materials company focused on advanced agricultural film solutions for modern farming.

Through its ANVIX™ product platform, the company serves the agriculture, horticulture, aquaculture, water-management and environmental sectors with high-performance film technologies designed for productivity, durability and sustainability.
