ANVIX™ Silage Solutions are designed to protect forage quality, reduce spoilage losses and improve storage efficiency across a wide range of silage applications.

From silage covers and oxygen barrier films to high-performance storage solutions, ANVIX™ products help minimise oxygen ingress, preserve nutritional value and support consistent feed quality throughout the storage period.

01
ANVIX™ Bale Wrapping Films
Secure Every Bale. Preserve Every Nutrient.
ANVIX™ Bale Wrapping Films are engineered to provide reliable airtight protection for round and square silage bales. Manufactured from high-performance polyethylene resins, the film combines exceptional stretchability, puncture resistance and cling performance to maintain bale integrity throughout storage.
The multi-layer film structure creates an effective oxygen barrier around the bale, supporting optimal anaerobic fermentation and helping preserve valuable nutrients. By minimizing oxygen ingress and reducing spoilage risks, ANVIX™ Bale Wrapping Films contribute to improved forage quality and long-term feed preservation.
Designed for demanding agricultural environments, the film offers excellent UV stability, consistent wrapping performance and secure bale sealing under varying weather conditions.

02
ANVIX™ Silage Bags
Flexible Storage. Maximum Preservation.
ANVIX™ Silage Bags are engineered from high-strength multi-layer polyethylene films designed to create a reliable airtight storage environment for both silage and grain applications.
The hermetic storage system minimizes oxygen ingress while maintaining stable internal conditions throughout the storage period. For silage applications, this promotes efficient anaerobic fermentation, helping preserve valuable nutrients, reduce dry matter losses and improve feed quality. For grain storage, the sealed environment naturally suppresses insect activity, mold growth and fungal development without the need for permanent storage facilities.
Manufactured with UV-stabilised virgin polyethylene resins, ANVIX™ Silage Bags deliver excellent puncture resistance, tear strength and weather durability under demanding agricultural conditions. The flexible storage format allows producers to expand storage capacity quickly while maintaining product quality from harvest through feeding or processing.
Key Features
  • High mechanical strength
  • Excellent oxygen barrier
  • Long-term outdoor durability
  • Suitable for forage and grain storage
03
ANVIX™ Oxygen Barrier Silage Cover
Engineered with advanced 7-layer co-extrusion technology, ANVIX™ Oxygen Barrier Silage Cover significantly reduces oxygen permeability compared with conventional PE silage films. The enhanced barrier structure helps maintain anaerobic fermentation conditions, reducing spoilage and preserving valuable nutrients within the silage.
Its lightweight yet high-strength construction allows easier handling and installation while maintaining excellent puncture and tear resistance. The flexible film structure conforms closely to the silage surface, minimizing air pockets and improving sealing performance.
By combining oxygen barrier protection and silage covering performance into a single recyclable film solution, ANVIX™ helps improve forage quality, reduce dry matter losses and support more efficient livestock feeding operations.
