Product Overview
Engineered for Long-Term Containment

ANVIX™ HDPE Geomembrane is manufactured from 100% virgin high-density polyethylene resin, compounded with carbon black, antioxidants and UV stabilisers to deliver a durable, chemically resistant lining material.

HDPE is suitable for applications requiring high tensile strength, rigidity and resistance to a broad range of chemicals — making it a preferred choice for permanent and semi-permanent containment structures where structural integrity and long-term performance are critical.

Every roll is produced under controlled manufacturing conditions to ensure consistent thickness, surface quality and mechanical performance in line with internationally recognised standards.

Quality Assurance
Standards & Testing
ANVIX™ HDPE Geomembrane is manufactured and tested in accordance with internationally recognised geosynthetic standards, ensuring consistent quality and reliable field performance.
GRI GM13
Geosynthetic Research Institute Specification
GM13 is the industry-recognised specification for HDPE geomembranes, covering minimum requirements for thickness, tensile properties, tear resistance, puncture resistance and carbon black content.
ASTM
ASTM Test Methods
Material properties are evaluated using established ASTM test methods, including tensile properties, tear resistance, puncture resistance, density and dimensional stability.
QA / QC
Quality Control Procedures
Each production batch is monitored for thickness uniformity, surface quality and mechanical properties to ensure consistent compliance with specification.
UV / OIT
Oxidative Induction Time Testing
OIT testing verifies the effectiveness of antioxidant packages, supporting long-term durability claims for outdoor installations.
